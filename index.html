Biography

I am a Data Scientist at the Children’s Hospital of Philadelphia (CHOP), where I develop applied artificial intelligence systems for healthcare research. My work focuses on clinical natural language processing, large language models, and computer vision methods for analyzing large-scale, real-world clinical and behavioral data.

Previously, I held research and engineering roles at A*STAR, Hitachi Asia, Nanyang Technological University, and the Indian Institute of Science. My research spans healthcare AI, autism research, smart cities, intelligent sensing, and theory-guided machine learning. I have published in international journals, conferences, and edited volumes, and have received multiple best paper awards.
