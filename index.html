About

I am a Data Scientist at the Children’s Hospital of Philadelphia (CHOP), where I work on applied artificial intelligence and machine learning methods for healthcare research. My work focuses on developing and validating AI systems that transform unstructured clinical data into structured, research-ready resources.

Previously, I held research and engineering roles at A*STAR, Hitachi Asia, and Nanyang Technological University. I hold a Master’s degree in Machine Learning & Computing and have published across international journals and conferences with over 100 citations.
