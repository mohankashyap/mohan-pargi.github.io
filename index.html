Biography

I am a Data Scientist at the Children’s Hospital of Philadelphia (CHOP), where I design and deploy applied artificial intelligence systems for healthcare research. My work focuses on clinical natural language processing, large language models, and computer vision methods that transform unstructured clinical data into structured, research-ready resources.

Prior to CHOP, I held research and engineering roles at A*STAR, Hitachi Asia, Nanyang Technological University, and the Indian Institute of Science. My work has spanned healthcare informatics, autism research, multilingual AI systems, smart city platforms, and intelligent sensing. I hold a Master’s degree in Machine Learning & Computing and have published across international journals and conferences.
