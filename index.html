Biography

I am a Data Scientist at the Children’s Hospital of Philadelphia (CHOP), where I develop applied artificial intelligence systems for healthcare research. My work focuses on clinical natural language processing, large language models, and computer vision methods that transform unstructured clinical data into structured, research-ready resources.

Previously, I held research and engineering roles at A*STAR, Hitachi Asia, Nanyang Technological University, and the Indian Institute of Science. I hold a Master’s degree in Machine Learning & Computing and have received multiple best paper awards at international conferences.
